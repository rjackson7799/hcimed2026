## What Is an Annual Wellness Visit?

The Annual Wellness Visit is a yearly appointment focused on prevention and creating a personalized health plan. Unlike a regular checkup or physical exam, the AWV is designed to:

- Review your health risk factors
- Create or update your personalized prevention plan
- Screen for cognitive impairment
- Review your medications and health history
- Discuss advance care planning

## What to Expect During Your Visit

When you arrive for your Annual Wellness Visit at HCI Medical Group, our team will:

### 1. Update Your Health History

We'll review any changes to your health since your last visit, including new diagnoses, hospitalizations, or specialist consultations.

### 2. Check Your Vital Signs

Your height, weight, blood pressure, and other measurements help us track changes in your health over time.

### 3. Assess Your Health Risks

We'll discuss factors like your family history, lifestyle habits, and current health conditions to identify potential risks.

### 4. Create Your Prevention Plan

Based on your individual needs, we'll recommend appropriate screenings, vaccinations, and preventive services for the coming year.

## First Visit vs. Subsequent Visits

**Initial Preventive Physical Examination (IPPE):** If you're new to Medicare, you're eligible for a one-time "Welcome to Medicare" visit within the first 12 months of enrollment.

**Annual Wellness Visit:** After your first year, you can schedule an AWV every 12 months to maintain your personalized prevention plan.

## How to Prepare

To get the most from your Annual Wellness Visit:

- Bring a list of all current medications, including over-the-counter drugs and supplements
- Write down any health concerns or questions you'd like to discuss
- Bring records of any care you've received from other providers
- Consider bringing a family member who can help with health history
