Choosing a new doctor is a big decision, and we want your first visit to HCI Medical Group to be comfortable, thorough, and reassuring. Here's what you can expect when you walk through our doors.

## Before Your Visit

To make the most of your appointment, please bring:

- **Photo ID** and your **insurance card**
- A **list of current medications**, including dosages and supplements
- **Recent medical records** or test results, if available
- A **list of questions** or health concerns you'd like to discuss

If you have specific forms from your insurance company that need to be completed, bring those along too.

## What Happens During Your First Appointment

Your first visit is longer than a typical follow-up—usually about 45 minutes to an hour. Here's what we'll cover:

### Health History Review

Your provider will ask about:

- Your medical history, including past surgeries, hospitalizations, and chronic conditions
- Family health history (heart disease, diabetes, cancer, etc.)
- Current symptoms or health concerns
- Lifestyle factors like diet, exercise, sleep, and stress

Be honest and thorough—this information helps us provide the best care for you.

### Physical Examination

Depending on your age and health history, your exam may include:

- Vital signs (blood pressure, heart rate, temperature, weight)
- Heart and lung examination
- Abdominal examination
- Basic neurological screening

### Creating Your Care Plan

After the exam, your provider will discuss:

- Any immediate health concerns and recommended next steps
- Preventive screenings you may be due for
- A plan for managing any chronic conditions
- When to schedule your next appointment

## What Sets Us Apart

At HCI Medical Group, you'll see the **same provider at every visit**. We believe continuity of care—building a real relationship with your doctor—leads to better outcomes and a better experience. You won't be shuffled between rotating providers.

We also pride ourselves on **same-week appointments**. When you need to be seen, we make time for you.
