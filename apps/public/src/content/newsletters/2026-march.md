## Schedule Your Annual Physical

When was your last physical? If you have to think about it, it's probably been too long — and that's okay. You're not alone, and there's no better time than now.

**Why annual physicals matter:**

- **Catch problems early** — Many conditions like high blood pressure, diabetes, and high cholesterol have no symptoms in the early stages. A physical can catch these before they become serious.
- **Update your screenings** — Depending on your age and health history, you may be due for important screenings that could save your life.
- **Build a relationship with your provider** — Regular visits help us understand your health over time and provide better, more personalized care.
- **Peace of mind** — Sometimes just knowing where you stand is the best medicine.

**Haven't had a physical in a while?** No judgment — just good care. Our team will make you feel comfortable and walk you through everything step by step. Whether it's been one year or ten, we're glad you're coming in.

Schedule your annual physical or wellness visit today. Your future self will thank you.
